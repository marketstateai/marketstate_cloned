# MarketState Elevator Pitch

## One-liner
MarketState is a paid investment operating system that helps active investors with multiple accounts see if they are on track, understand portfolio risk clearly, and take better next actions.

## 30-second pitch
Most self-directed investors have money spread across multiple brokers, pensions, and crypto apps, but no single system that tells them if they are actually progressing toward a long-term target. MarketState consolidates portfolio visibility, overlays clear risk and performance context, and turns that into actionable alerts and review loops. We are building a focused $10/month product for active investors who already pay for financial tools and want better decisions, not more noise.

## 60-second pitch
MarketState is built for active investors who have fragmented portfolios and weak feedback loops. Today, they can see balances, but they cannot easily answer the questions that matter: Am I on track? Where is my risk concentrated? What should I change now?

We solve this with a goal-driven investor operating system: one consolidated portfolio view, risk and performance insight tied to long-term objectives, and alert-driven workflows that prompt action. The model is simple: paid subscription from day one at $10/month, focused on users with 2+ accounts who already spend on financial tooling. The opportunity is to become the default decision layer between market noise and real portfolio behavior.

## 2-minute investor pitch
Self-directed investors are growing in number, but their workflow is still broken. They use multiple brokerages, pension portals, and finance apps, then patch everything together in spreadsheets and mental models. Existing products either act like broad personal-finance hubs or brokerage-centric dashboards that optimize for activity.

MarketState is positioned differently: we are the investor decision layer. We connect fragmented data, map it to a user-defined long-term objective, and show three things clearly: current position, risk concentration, and what to do next. The MVP is intentionally narrow: unified portfolio dashboard, watchlists and threshold alerts, risk analytics, monthly report exports, and subscription/entitlement gating.

We are targeting active investors first because they feel the pain now and pay for outcomes now. Pricing is straightforward at $10/month. Validation target is 2,000 paid users in year one ($20k MRR), with a longer path toward 100k paid users. This is not a brokerage replacement; it is a repeat-use SaaS product focused on better investor decisions and high retention through a weekly review loop.

## Customer-facing version
If you invest across multiple apps and accounts, MarketState gives you one clear system to track progress toward your goal, understand your real risk, and know what to adjust next.
