# MarketState Pitch Deck

## Deck Goal
Secure investor confidence by proving three things:
1. The problem is painful and validated.
2. The solution is differentiated and executable.
3. The business can become a durable recurring-revenue company.

## Deck Length
- Target: 10-12 slides
- Live delivery: 8-12 minutes

---

## Slide 1: Cover
**Title:** MarketState  
**Subtitle:** The investment operating system for active investors

### Key message
We help multi-account investors make better long-term decisions with one goal-driven system.

### Visual
- Brand mark + clean statement
- Tagline only, no dense text

### Speaker notes
Open with your one-sentence thesis and the exact audience you serve first.

---

## Slide 2: Problem
**Title:** Investors are fragmented, reactive, and under-informed

### Key points
- Portfolios are split across broker apps, pensions, and crypto accounts.
- Existing tools focus on activity or static reporting, not decisions.
- Users can’t confidently answer: "Am I on track and what should I change?"

### Visual
- Before-state workflow map (many apps -> confusion)

### Speaker notes
Keep this concrete and emotional. Show the decision-cost of fragmentation.

---

## Slide 3: Solution
**Title:** A goal-driven investor command center

### Key points
- Unified portfolio and net-worth visibility.
- Risk and performance context tied to long-term goals.
- Alert and review loops that push timely action.

### Visual
- Product concept diagram: Inputs -> Intelligence -> Actions

### Speaker notes
Frame this as a decision system, not just another tracker.

---

## Slide 4: Product (MVP)
**Title:** We ship the smallest product that closes the loop

### MVP scope
- Unified dashboard (positions, allocation, PnL)
- Watchlists + threshold alerts
- Risk analytics (concentration, drawdown, volatility)
- Monthly report export
- Paid subscription + entitlements

### Out of scope (for now)
- Full AI advisor
- Brokerage replacement
- Social feed/community platform

### Speaker notes
Investors should hear prioritization discipline.
